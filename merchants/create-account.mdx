title: "Create Your Account"
description: "How to sign up for Qtap and get your merchant dashboard ready."
---

Qtap accounts start at [dashboard.qtap.qa](https://dashboard.qtap.qa). Signing up creates your organization and an owner login for the dashboard, and puts the account on a trial. The form itself takes a couple of minutes. Setting up your loyalty program after signup takes longer.

## Before you sign up

Have these ready:

- A working email address you check often. Qtap sends a verification link there and uses it for billing and password resets.
- Your business name as you want it to appear to customers in the Qtap mobile app, plus a phone number for your business.

You do not need a credit card to sign up. Qtap starts every account on a trial so you can build your program before you pick a plan.

## Sign up steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the signup page">
    Go to [dashboard.qtap.qa](https://dashboard.qtap.qa) and click **Sign up** at the bottom of the login box. If you already have an invite link from a Qtap team member, use that link instead so you land on the right form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your business details">
    Fill in your business name, your email, a phone number, and a password. Your business name shows up on stamp cards and campaign messages, so use the name customers will recognize.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Qtap sends a verification link to the address you entered. Open it and click the link. If it does not arrive within a minute, check your spam folder or click **Resend verification** on the login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in and land on the onboarding wizard">
    After verifying, log in with the email and password you just set. Qtap drops you into the onboarding wizard. That is where you pick a loyalty type and set up your first location. The wizard has its own walkthrough page linked at the bottom.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The email on your account is the only one that can reset your password or change billing details. If you plan to share access with other people at your business, do not reuse a personal email. Use a shared address like owner@yourbusiness.com so you do not lose access if someone leaves.
</Warning>

## What happens after signup

Your account starts on a trial with the Growth plan's features unlocked. During the trial you can build stamp cards, invite staff, generate QR codes, and enroll members. When the trial ends, pick a plan in **Settings > Billing** to keep the account active. Anything you created during the trial stays in place after you subscribe.

A Getting Started checklist shows at the top of the dashboard to guide you through the first setup tasks. See [Welcome to Qtap](/merchants/welcome) for what each checklist item does.

<Note>
One email address can own one Qtap organization at a time. If you run more than one business and want a separate Qtap account for each, sign up with a different email for each organization.
</Note>

## Forgot your password

Click **Forgot password** on the login page and enter the email on your account. Qtap emails a reset link. The link works once and expires after a short window, so use it right away. If the reset email does not arrive, check spam or contact support at support@qtap.qa.

## Deleting an account

You cannot delete an organization from the dashboard. If you need to close your account, email support@qtap.qa from the owner email address and ask for deletion. Export anything you want to keep first, because member lists and analytics go away with the organization.
