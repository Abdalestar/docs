title: "Location Comparison"
description: "Compare stamps issued, redemptions, and member growth across your business locations."
---

The Location Comparison view shows side-by-side numbers for every location on your account. Use it to spot which branches are performing well and which need a push. Go to **Analytics** in the left sidebar, then open the **Locations** tab at the top of the page.

<Note>
  You'll only see this tab if you have two or more active locations. If you have one location, the regular dashboard already shows everything.
</Note>

## What the view shows

For each location, the table shows:

- **Total Members** — members who joined at this location.
- **Active Members (30d)** — members who scanned a QR code or earned a stamp here in the last 30 days.
- **Stamps Issued** or **Points Issued** — depending on your loyalty type.
- **Redemptions** — rewards redeemed at this location.
- **Avg. Visits per Member** — how often members come back.

The location with the highest number in each column is highlighted.

## Changing the time period

The period selector at the top right of the page filters every column. Options are:

- **7d** — last 7 days
- **30d** — last 30 days (default)
- **90d** — last 90 days
- **All** — since you opened the account

## Reading the comparison

Look for two things:

1. **Members per location.** A location with low numbers might need more visible QR codes or a flyer at the counter.
2. **Redemptions vs. stamps issued.** A location issuing lots of stamps but few redemptions usually means members aren't coming back to redeem. A win-back campaign helps here.

## Filtering by loyalty card

If you run more than one card, the **Card** dropdown above the table filters the comparison to a single card. This is useful when one card is location-specific.

## Exporting the comparison

Click **Export** at the top right to download the table as a CSV file. The file uses the same time period and card filter you have selected.
