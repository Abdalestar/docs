---
title: "Staff Performance"
description: "See which staff are issuing stamps and processing redemptions."
---

The Staff Performance view ranks every staff member on your account by activity. Use it to spot training gaps and to recognise the staff who keep customers coming back. Go to **Analytics** in the left sidebar and open the **Staff** tab.

## What you'll see

The page is one bar chart and one table.

### Bar chart

The chart ranks staff by **stamps issued** (or **points issued**, if you run a points program). The staff member with the most activity is at the top.

### Staff table

For each staff member, the table shows:

- **Name** — first and last name from their profile.
- **Role** — Owner, Manager, or Staff.
- **Locations** — which locations they work at.
- **Stamps Issued** or **Points Issued** — in the selected period.
- **Redemptions Processed** — rewards they marked as redeemed.
- **Last Active** — the last time they used the dashboard or scanner.

## Time period

The period selector at the top right of the page filters the chart and table. Options are **7d**, **30d**, **90d**, and **All**. The default is 30d.

## What the numbers tell you

A few patterns to watch for:

- **A staff member at zero across the board.** They probably haven't been trained on the scanner, or they're a new starter. Show them the [Staff Workflow](/merchants/redemptions/staff-workflow) page.
- **Stamps issued but no redemptions processed.** They're stamping customers but never running redemption when those customers come back to claim a reward. Walk through the redemption screen with them.
- **Heavy use at one location, none at another.** If a staff member is assigned to two locations but only working one, you can shrink their location assignments under **Staff → [staff member] → Edit**.

## Per-staff drill-down

Click a row in the table to open that staff member's detail page. You'll see a daily breakdown of their stamps, redemptions, and login history.

## Exporting

Click **Export** at the top right. The export covers the period you selected and downloads as CSV.
