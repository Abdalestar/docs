The Analytics overview page shows aggregate numbers for your loyalty program. The detailed reports go further, letting you look at the underlying data for specific parts of your operation.

Each report has the same time period selector in the top right corner. Pick **7 days**, **30 days**, **90 days**, or **12 months** to control the date range for the data on the page.

## Points Activity

A complete transaction log for your points program. Every time a member earns, redeems, adjusts, or expires points, a row is recorded here. Each entry shows the member's name, the date and time, a type badge (Earned, Redeemed, Adjusted, or Expired), the number of points, the associated purchase amount if there was one, and a description.

The table shows 50 rows per page. Use the pagination controls at the bottom to move through older records.

</Card>

## Revenue Impact

This report correlates your loyalty program activity with revenue over time. At the top, three summary numbers give you the totals for the selected period: total revenue, number of loyalty transactions, and the average revenue per transaction.

Below those, a dual-axis line chart plots revenue on the left axis and transaction volume on the right. You can see whether periods of high loyalty activity line up with higher sales, or spot gaps that might be worth looking into.

<Card title="Revenue Impact Report" icon="dollar-sign" href="/merchants/analytics/revenue-impact">
  Revenue totals and transaction counts on one dual-axis chart
</Card>

## Staff Performance

A ranked table of your staff members showing what each one did in the selected period: stamps issued, points issued, rewards processed, members served, and daily averages for stamps and points. If you notice one person's numbers are well below everyone else's, that is a conversation worth having.

<Card title="Staff Performance Report" icon="users" href="/merchants/analytics/staff-performance">
  Per-staff breakdown of stamps, points, rewards, and daily averages
</Card>

## Location Comparison

If you run more than one location, this report puts them side by side. Each location gets its own card with five numbers: stamps issued, points issued, redemptions, new members, and estimated revenue for the period.

Only locations that had at least one scan during the selected period appear. A branch with no loyalty activity during that window won't show up.
