Revenue Impact is the dashboard's estimate of how much extra business your loyalty program brings in. It's not exact accounting, it's a useful picture of trends. Go to **Analytics** in the left sidebar and open the **Revenue Impact** tab.

<Note>
  Revenue numbers depend on you setting an **item value** on each reward when you create it. If you skip the item value, that reward shows as $0 in the report.
</Note>

## How the number is calculated

Qtap multiplies redemptions by the item value you set on each reward, then sums it up. For example:

- 30 redemptions of a Free Coffee at $4 each = $120 in redeemed value.
- 10 redemptions of a Free Pastry at $3 each = $30 in redeemed value.
- Revenue Impact = $150 for the period.

This is the value you've given away. The point of the loyalty program is that the visits earning those rewards are worth more than the rewards themselves.

## What you'll see on the page

### Total Revenue Impact (est.)

The headline number for the period you selected. The label says **est.** because it's an estimate based on the item values you entered, not your real receipts.

### Revenue by Reward

Bar chart showing which rewards drove the most redemption value. The reward at the top is the one members value the most.

### Revenue by Location

If you have multiple locations, this bar chart breaks the impact down by location. It's the same numbers as the Location Comparison view, filtered to revenue.

### Revenue by Campaign

If you've run any campaigns with rewards attached, this chart shows how much of the impact came from each campaign. Use it to decide which campaigns to repeat.

## Setting accurate item values

The report is only as good as the values you enter. To check or fix a reward's value:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the reward">
    Go to **Cards** in the left sidebar, click your card, then click the reward in the Rewards section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Item Value">
    In the reward editor, find the **Item Value** field and enter the menu price of the item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. New redemptions of this reward use the updated value. Past redemptions keep the old value.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Exporting the report

Click **Export** at the top right of the page. You can download as CSV (for spreadsheets) or PDF (for sharing).
