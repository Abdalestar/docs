This page is for points programs. It shows how points are flowing in and out across all your members. Go to **Analytics** in the left sidebar, then open the **Points** tab. The tab only appears if you have at least one points program.

<Note>
  If you only run stamp cards, you won't see this view. The equivalent for stamps is in [Analytics Overview](/merchants/analytics/overview) under "Stamps Activity Over Time."
</Note>

## Metrics at the top

Four numbers run across the top of the page:

- **Total Points Issued** — every point ever given out.
- **Total Points Redeemed** — every point spent on a reward.
- **Average Points per Member** — the typical balance a member is sitting on.
- **Points Expiring Soon** — points that will expire in the next 30 days, if you've turned on expiry.

## Charts on the page

### Points Earned Over Time

Line chart of points issued per day in your selected time period. A flat line for several days usually means a slow week or a problem with your QR codes (members can't earn what they can't scan).

### Points Balance Trend

Area chart showing the average member's balance over time. A balance that keeps growing means members are earning but not redeeming, which usually points to rewards being too expensive or hard to spot in the app.

### Rewards Popularity

Bar chart of which point rewards are redeemed most often. The reward at the top of the chart is your most valuable one to keep stocked. Rewards with zero redemptions over 30 days are candidates to retire or replace.

## Changing the time period

Use the period selector at the top right: **7d**, **30d**, **90d**, or **All**. The default is 30d.

## Spotting issues

A few patterns worth catching:

- **Issued goes up, redeemed stays flat.** Members are earning but not spending. Check that your rewards are reachable from the app and not priced too high.
- **Issued and redeemed both drop.** Visits are down. Try a win-back campaign or a points multiplier weekend.
- **Expiring Soon is a big number.** Members will lose points unless they visit. Send a notification reminding them.
