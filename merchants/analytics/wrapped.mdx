"Your Wrapped" shows how your loyalty program performed over a completed period. You get six numbers (stamps, points, redemptions, new members, estimated revenue, growth rate), your most active customer, and your busiest day of the week.

## Opening Wrapped

Go to **Analytics** in the left sidebar and choose **Your Wrapped**. You'll see three tabs: **Weekly**, **Monthly**, and **Yearly**.

Each tab covers the most recently completed period, not the one currently in progress. If you open the Weekly tab on a Wednesday, you're seeing last week's numbers (Sunday to Saturday), not the current partial week.

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|---------------|
| Weekly | Previous Sunday to Saturday |
| Monthly | Previous full calendar month |
| Yearly | Previous full calendar year |

## What's on the page

**Your Numbers** lists six figures: total stamps issued, points earned, redemptions, new members, estimated revenue, and growth rate. Growth rate is the number of new members divided by your total membership at the start of the period. An account that had 200 members at the start of the week and gained 20 shows a 10% growth rate.

**Highlights** writes the same data as plain sentences: "342 stamps issued" or "Tuesday was your busiest day." Up to five highlights appear depending on what activity you had.

**Top Customer** shows the member who visited most during the period, with their name and visit count. The card only appears when there was activity to rank.

**Busiest Day** shows the weekday with the most combined stamp and points activity. It also only appears when data exists.

## Plan requirement

Wrapped is on Growth, Elite, and Franchise plans. If you're on Starter, the page shows an upgrade prompt instead of data. Your current plan is under **Settings > Billing**.

<Info>
Revenue figures are estimates. Qtap does not process payments, so the revenue number comes from your redemption activity, not actual transaction amounts.
</Info>

## If no data appears

A new account or a period with no scans or redemptions shows "No data available for this period yet." Switch to the Monthly or Yearly tab — those cover longer windows and may have older activity to show.
