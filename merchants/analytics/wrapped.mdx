Analytics Wrapped compiles everything that happened in your loyalty program over a completed time window and presents it as a clean summary. Open it when you want a quick picture of a week, a month, or a full year without digging through charts.

<Info>
  Wrapped is available on the **Growth plan and above**. If you're on the Starter plan, the page shows a lock screen with a link to upgrade at **Settings → Billing**.
</Info>

## Opening Wrapped

In the dashboard sidebar, go to **Analytics**, then click **Wrapped**. The page loads showing your last complete week by default.

## Choosing a period

Three tabs sit at the top:

- **Weekly** — the last complete Sunday through Saturday
- **Monthly** — the last full calendar month
- **Yearly** — the last full calendar year

Switching tabs reloads the summary for that window. Your current week, month, or year is not shown because the data is not final yet.

## What you see

Each period has up to four cards.

**Your Numbers**

A two-column grid with six figures from the period:

| Metric | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| Total Stamps | Stamps issued across all your locations |
| Points Earned | Points added to member balances |
| Redemptions | Rewards redeemed by members |
| New Members | Members who joined during the period |
| Est. Revenue | An estimate based on activity logged by the system |
| Growth | New members as a percentage of your member count at the start of the period |

The Growth percentage can read low or zero for new businesses. If you had 10 members at the start of a week and gained 1, Growth shows 10%.

**Highlights**

Plain-English bullets summarizing what stood out — total stamps, points earned, new joins, your most active customer, and your busiest day. The bullets only appear for metrics that have a non-zero value, so a slow week might have only two or three lines.

**Top Customer**

The member with the most logged activity in the period, along with a count of their visits. This card only appears when the system has activity data for at least one member.

**Busiest Day**

The day of the week that had the highest combined stamp and point activity. This card only appears when the period has enough daily data to rank days.

## Sharing your summary

A **Share** button sits in the top right of each period view. Use it to export or copy the Wrapped summary for your team or social media.

## No data yet

If a period shows "No data available for this period yet," nothing was recorded in that window. This is normal for:

- New accounts where data collection has just started
- The Yearly tab early in a calendar year (the previous year may have no data if the account is recent)
- Any week or month before you enrolled your first member

The page will populate automatically once activity is logged.

<Tip>
  The Analytics overview at **Analytics → Overview** shows your live data with time period filters. Use it for day-to-day monitoring. Use Wrapped for a finished-period snapshot you can share.
</Tip>
