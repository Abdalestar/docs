Every Qtap business gets a public page at `qtap.qa/m/your-slug`. It's what a customer sees when they scan your code or find you in the app: your cover photos, logo, a short description, your location, and the loyalty cards they can join on the spot. You build that page in the **Merchant Page** editor.

Open it from **Merchant Page** in the sidebar. The left column holds the fields you fill in; the right column shows a live preview of the page as customers will see it, updating as you type.

<Warning>
Only the account owner can open the Merchant Page editor. Managers and staff see an access-denied message.
</Warning>

## Set your merchant profile

The Merchant Profile card holds everything at the top of your page.

- **Merchant Name**: your business name as customers read it.
- **Cover Photos**: up to five photos in PNG or JPEG, 5MB each. The first photo is your main banner; drag the rest to reorder them. Hover a photo to make it the main cover, replace it, or remove it.
- **Merchant Logo**: a square image (PNG or JPEG, up to 10MB) shown next to your name.
- **Merchant Logo (Transparent PNG)**: a 512x512 PNG with a transparent background, up to 10MB. Qtap uses this one as the stamp icon on your mobile loyalty cards, so a clean transparent version is worth uploading.
- **Description**: a line or two about your business.
- **Google Rating**: a star value you set with the slider. You enter this yourself; it does not pull from Google.
- **Category**: choose one of Restaurants, Cafes, Retail, Beauty, Health, Services, Entertainment, or Other.

## Add your location details

Below the profile, fill in where customers find you: **Location Name**, **Address**, **Phone Number**, **Working Hours** (free text, so write it however reads best), and your **Website URL**.

## Feature your loyalty cards

The Loyalty Cards section lists the stamp cards and points programs you've already published. Tap one to feature it on your page. These are your real cards, so a customer who opens your page can join and start collecting right away.

Only active, published programs show up here. If the list is empty, use **Create a loyalty card** first, publish it, then come back and feature it.

## Preview, then publish

**Preview** opens your page in a new tab with your current edits, including changes you haven't saved yet, so you can check it before it goes live.

When it looks right, click **Create Merchant** to publish. After the first save the button reads **Update Merchant**, which you use for every later change.

<Frame caption="Preview opens your page in a new tab. Create Merchant publishes it; after that the button becomes Update Merchant.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/merchant-page/building-04-publish.png" alt="The Create Merchant and Preview buttons at the bottom of the editor" />
</Frame>

<Note>
Once your page is published, a **Delete** button appears. It resets the page back to a blank state by clearing your cover photos, logo, and these settings. It does not touch your loyalty programs or your members, so you can rebuild the page without losing anything else.
</Note>

<Tip>
The live preview on the right only shows on a wide screen. On a laptop or desktop you can edit and check the result side by side; on a narrow window, use **Preview** instead.
</Tip>
