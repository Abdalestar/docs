Staff members are the employees you invite to use the Qtap dashboard or mobile app at your locations. Each staff member gets their own login and a role that controls what they can access.

<Frame caption="The Staff page: invite a new teammate (1) or open the activity log (2). Each row shows a member's role, status, and join date.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/staff/01-staff-overview.png" alt="Staff page with the Invite Staff and Activity Log buttons highlighted" />
</Frame>

## Roles

There are two roles in Qtap:

**Manager** — can access all locations and all sections of the dashboard. Managers can view analytics, manage campaigns, and see member data across the entire organization.

**Staff** — has limited access by default. You control which locations they can work at and what actions they can take. A staff member might be able to issue stamps but not view analytics or export member data.

<Note>
The account owner has full access to everything, including billing and settings. The owner role cannot be assigned to invited staff.
</Note>

## What's on the Staff page

Go to **Staff** in the left sidebar to see your full staff list. Each row shows:

- The staff member's name and email
- Their role (Manager or Staff)
- Their status (Active or Pending)
- The date they were added

**Pending** means the invitation email has been sent but the staff member hasn't accepted it yet.

## Inviting a staff member

1. On the Staff page, click **Invite Staff Member**.
2. Fill in their first name, last name (optional), and email address.
3. Select their role: **Manager** or **Staff**.
4. If the role is Staff, select which locations they can access.
5. Click **Send Invite**.

The staff member receives an email with a link to create their account. Until they accept, their status shows as Pending.

<Frame caption="The invite form: enter the teammate's name and email, pick a role, and choose which locations they can access.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/staff/02-invite-dialog.png" alt="Invite Team Member dialog with name, email, and role fields" />
</Frame>

If the invite email didn't arrive, click the three-dot menu next to their row and select **Resend Invite**.

## Removing a staff member

Click the three-dot menu next to the staff member's row and select **Remove**. This immediately revokes their access. Their historical activity (stamps issued, etc.) remains in your records.

<Frame caption="Each teammate's row menu: edit their permissions, or remove their access.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/staff/03-row-menu.png" alt="Staff row menu showing Edit Permissions and Remove options" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
Removing a staff member cannot be undone. If they need access again, you'll need to send a new invite.
</Warning>

## Staff permissions

For staff with the Staff role, you can customize which sections of the dashboard they can access. Click the three-dot menu next to their name and select **Edit Permissions**. This opens the permissions dialog where you can grant or restrict access to members, analytics, and other sections.

<Frame caption="The Edit Permissions dialog. Turn on Use Custom Permissions to override the role defaults for one teammate.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/staff/04-edit-permissions.png" alt="Edit Permissions dialog with the Use Custom Permissions toggle highlighted" />
</Frame>

Managers always have full access and cannot have their permissions restricted.

## Plan limits

Your plan determines how many staff members you can invite. If you've reached the limit, the **Invite Staff Member** button won't be available. Go to **Settings > Billing** to upgrade.
