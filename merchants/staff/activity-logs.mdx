The activity log records everything your team does in the dashboard. Open it to see a timestamped history of staff actions and the customers they involved. If something happened in your account, it's here.

## Open the log

Go to **Staff** in the sidebar. At the top of the page, click **Activity Log**.

<Frame caption="The Activity Log button sits at the top of your Staff page.">
  <img src="/images/staff/staff-list-activity-button-final.png" alt="Staff list page showing the Activity Log button in the header" />
</Frame>

## What each row shows

The log table has five columns:

| Column | What it contains |
|--------|------------------|
| Date & Time | When the action happened, in your local time |
| Staff | The team member who took the action ("System" for automated events) |
| Action | What happened: a badge like "Stamp Issued" or "Reward Redeemed" |
| Member | The customer involved, if any |
| Details | Any extra data recorded with the event |

The log shows the 200 most recent entries across your whole account. If you need to go further back, use the date range filter to narrow your search.

<Frame caption="The staff activity log with recent actions listed.">
  <img src="/images/staff/activity-log-final.png" alt="Staff activity log showing a table of recent staff actions" />
</Frame>

## Filter the log

The filter bar above the table has four controls:

- **Staff Member**: show only one team member's actions
- **Action Type**: limit by event type (Stamp Issued, Points Issued, Reward Redeemed, and others)
- **From**: start of the date range
- **To**: end of the date range

You can combine filters. To remove them all at once, click **Clear**.

<Frame caption="Filters applied to show a specific staff member's stamp actions.">
  <img src="/images/staff/activity-log-filtered-final.png" alt="Activity log with Staff Member and Action Type filters applied" />
</Frame>

## Export to CSV

Click **Export CSV** at the top of the page to download the current view as a spreadsheet. The file includes a header section with your organization name and the export date, then one row per entry.

The button is greyed out if no entries match your current filters. Clear or adjust the filters before exporting if you see this.

<Info>
The export reflects whatever filters are active when you click it. To export your full history (up to 200 entries), clear all filters first.
</Info>

## Action types in the log

| Action | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| Stamp Issued | A staff member added stamps to a member's card |
| Points Issued | A staff member awarded points to a member |
| Reward Redeemed | A reward was processed at the counter |
| Member Joined | A customer enrolled in your loyalty program |
| Member Visit | A returning member checked in or scanned |
| Campaign Sent | A campaign message went out to members |
| Campaign Redeemed | A member redeemed an offer from a campaign |
| Notification Sent | A push notification was dispatched |

## Who can access this page

Owners and managers can view the full activity log. Staff members cannot access it.
