From the **Cards** page, click any card's preview image to open it. You can also open the three-dot menu next to a card and choose **Edit**. Both take you to the edit page.

The header shows the card's name and a status badge. Below that is the same four-step wizard you used to create the card, pre-filled with the current settings. On wider screens, a mobile preview appears on the right side and updates as you type so you can check how changes look in the app before saving.

## Making changes

Move between the four steps using **Next** and **Back**, or click any step in the progress bar at the top to jump to it directly.

The steps work the same way as during creation. See [Creating a Stamp Card](/merchants/stamp-cards/creating) for a full walkthrough of each field. The [Designing Your Stamp Card](/merchants/stamp-cards/designing) article covers the **Advanced Settings** panel in detail: stamping delays, expiry rules, bonus stamps, and partial redemption are all documented there.

When you reach the Review step, you have two options:

- **Save as Draft** saves your changes and sets the card to Draft. A draft card is not visible to customers and cannot be stamped.
- **Publish** saves your changes and sets the card to Active. If the card was already Active, it stays Active with the updated settings.

After saving, the page returns to the Cards list.

<Info>
The **Save Draft** button at the bottom of any step works without going to the Review step first.
</Info>

## Card status

A stamp card is always in one of three states. You change the status from the three-dot menu on the **Cards** list.

<Frame caption="Solid arrows are triggered from the edit wizard. Dashed arrows are from the Cards list menu.">
  <img src="/images/stamp-cards/card-status-flow.svg" alt="Diagram showing transitions between Draft, Active, and Inactive states" />
</Frame>

| Status | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Draft** | Not visible to customers. Staff cannot stamp it. |
| **Active** | Live. Customers can join, earn stamps, and redeem rewards. |
| **Inactive** | Paused. No new stamps can be issued. Existing members keep their stamp count and can still redeem earned rewards. |

To deactivate an active card: open the three-dot menu on the Cards page and choose **Deactivate**.

To reactivate: choose **Activate**.

To put an inactive card back into draft for further editing: choose **Convert to Draft**.

<Warning>
Deactivating a card does not delete stamp history. Members keep all their existing stamps.
</Warning>

## Duplicating a card

Open the three-dot menu and choose **Duplicate**. Qtap creates a draft copy with the same settings, name (appended with " (Copy)"), and rewards. The copy starts in Draft so you can edit it before activating.

## Deleting a card

Open the three-dot menu and choose **Delete**. A confirmation dialog appears before anything is removed. Deletion is permanent and removes the card from all member accounts.
