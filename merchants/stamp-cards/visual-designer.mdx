The Card Designer is a standalone visual tool, separate from the stamp card creation wizard. Use it to try different looks before creating a card. A phone preview on the right updates as you change settings, showing what your customers will see in the Qtap app.

To open it, go to **Stamp Cards** in the left sidebar and click **Card Designer**.

## Start with a template

The designer opens on the Templates tab. Six starting points cover common business types: Coffee Shop, Pizza Place, Salon & Spa, Retail Store, Gaming Cafe, and Blank. Clicking one sets the background colour, text colour, accent colour, stamp icon, and stamp count in one step. You can adjust any of those individually in the other three tabs.

## Colours

The Colors tab has three pickers: background colour, text colour, and accent colour. Background is the main card colour. Text colour applies to your business name and reward message; accent colour fills the completed stamp circles.

Click any colour swatch to open the picker. Drag the gradient or type a hex code directly into the field. Eight preset swatches appear at the bottom for quick choices.

## Stamp icon

The Icons tab shows 22 icons grouped by type: food and drinks, services, shopping, entertainment, and symbols. The selected icon appears inside each stamp circle. The preview updates as soon as you click.

## Layout and text

The Layout tab controls the stamp grid arrangement and the text on the card.

For stamp layout, choose Grid, Horizontal, or Vertical. Grid is the default and fits most card sizes.

The stamps-to-collect dropdown (6 to 20) controls how many circles appear in the preview. This is a visual setting only. The actual stamp goal a customer needs to earn the reward is set in the creation wizard.

Font has six options: Inter, Poppins, Roboto, Open Sans, Montserrat, and Playfair Display. Playfair Display suits salons and boutiques; the others work for most businesses.

Reward text is the short message at the bottom of the card. The default is "Get a FREE reward!" Change it to describe your actual offer, like "Buy 9, get 1 free!"

## Save and continue

When the design looks right, click **Save & Continue** in the top right. The designer passes your choices to the stamp card creation wizard, where you fill in the remaining details. The visual design carries across automatically.

<Warning>
The Card Designer does not save on its own. If you close the tab or leave the page before clicking Save & Continue, your changes are gone. Finish the wizard in the same browser session.
</Warning>
