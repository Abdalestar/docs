You can change a stamp card after you publish it. The editor uses the same wizard you used to build the card, so everything you set up is there to adjust.

## Open a card for editing

Go to **Cards** in the left sidebar. Each card has a menu (the three dots at the top-right of the card). Open it and choose **Edit**. Clicking the card itself opens the same editor.

<Frame caption="Open a card's menu and choose Edit to open it for editing.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/stamp-cards/editing-01-menu.png" alt="The Stamp Cards page with a card menu open and the Edit item highlighted" />
</Frame>

The page is titled **Edit Stamp Card**. A status badge next to the title shows whether the card is **Active**, **Inactive**, or a **Draft**.

## What you can change

The editor is the same four-step wizard from setup: Card Design, Rewards, Locations, and Review. Every field is filled in with the card's current settings. You can change the name, stamp goal, card color, stamp icon, description, rewards, valid locations, and the advanced stamping rules.

A live preview of the card sits on the right and updates as you type. The **With stamps / Completed** toggle above it lets you preview both states.

<Frame caption="The editor loads the card's current settings, with a live preview on the right.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/stamp-cards/editing-02-edit-page.png" alt="The Edit Stamp Card page showing the pre-filled Card Design step and a live preview" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
Each time you save, Qtap replaces the card's rewards with whatever is in the **Rewards** step at that moment. If you remove a reward there and save, that reward is gone. Check that the Rewards step lists every reward you want to keep before you save.
</Warning>

## Save your changes

The **Review** step checks that everything required is filled in, then gives you two buttons.

<Frame caption="On Review, Publish Card keeps the card live; Save as Draft takes it offline.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/stamp-cards/editing-03-review.png" alt="The Review step with the Save as Draft and Publish Card buttons highlighted" />
</Frame>

- **Publish Card** saves your changes and keeps the card live.
- **Save as Draft** saves your changes but sets the card back to Draft. A draft card is offline, so customers can't see it or collect stamps on it until you publish it again.

To edit a live card without taking it down, finish at Review and click **Publish Card**. Use **Save as Draft** only when you want to pull the card while you keep working on it.

## Turn a card on or off without editing

If you only want to stop or resume a card, you don't need the editor. On the Cards page, open the card's menu and choose **Deactivate** (or **Activate**). Deactivating stops new stamps without deleting the card or its history.

## Who can edit cards

Owners and managers can edit cards by default. Staff don't have access to the Cards area unless you grant it in their permissions.
