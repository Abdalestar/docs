You can change a stamp card after it goes live. The same wizard you used to build the card reopens with every field filled in, so you only adjust what you want and save.

## Open a card for editing

Go to **Cards** in the left sidebar. Each card on the Stamp Cards page shows its name, status, and stamp progress. To edit one, click the card itself, or open its three-dot menu and choose **Edit**.

<Frame caption="The Stamp Cards page. Click a card, or its three-dot menu and then Edit, to open it.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/stamp-cards/editing-01-open.png" alt="The Stamp Cards page showing three cards, with one card highlighted as the way to open it for editing" />
</Frame>

This opens the edit screen, titled **Edit Stamp Card** with the card's current status next to it (Active, Draft, or Inactive). It is the same four-step wizard as card creation, with Card Design, Rewards, Locations, and Review, and a live preview of the member's card on the right.

<Frame caption="The edit screen reopens the wizard with the card's current settings already filled in.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/stamp-cards/editing-02-edit-screen.png" alt="The Edit Stamp Card screen showing the Card Design step pre-filled with the card's name, stamp goal, and description next to a live preview" />
</Frame>

## Change what you need

Because the card is already complete, you can click any step in the bar at the top to jump straight to it. Every field works the same way it did when you first built the card:

- **Card Design** sets the name, stamp goal, colors, icon, and the front and back text. See [Designing your stamp card](/merchants/stamp-cards/designing).
- **Rewards** holds the Main, Sign Up, and Interim rewards. See [Stamp card rewards](/merchants/stamp-cards/rewards).
- **Locations** controls which branches can stamp the card.
- **Advanced Settings**, below the Card Design step, holds the stamping delay, daily cap, expiry, and bonus stamps. See [Advanced settings](/merchants/stamp-cards/advanced-settings).

<Note>
Editing changes the card's settings and rewards from this point on. It does not erase the stamps your members have already collected. If you lower the stamp goal, members who already have that many stamps reach the reward sooner.
</Note>

## Save your changes

Open the **Review** step to save. It shows a summary of the settings and rewards so you can check them first.

<Frame caption="The Review step summarizes your changes. Publish Card keeps the card live; Save as Draft turns it back into a draft.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/stamp-cards/editing-03-save.png" alt="The Review step with the Save as Draft and Publish Card buttons highlighted" />
</Frame>

- **Publish Card** saves your changes and makes the card live (or keeps it live). Members see the update in the app.
- **Save as Draft** saves your changes but sets the card to Draft.

<Warning>
If you are editing a card that is currently **Active** and you click **Save as Draft**, the card becomes a draft and disappears from the member app until you publish it again. To keep a live card live after editing, use **Publish Card**.
</Warning>

<Note>
Saving rewrites the card's reward list. Whatever rewards are in the Rewards step when you save become the card's rewards, so remove a reward there only if you want it gone, and make sure every reward you want to keep is still listed before you save.
</Note>

## Just need to pause or copy a card?

You do not have to open the editor for a status change. From the three-dot menu on the Cards page you can **Deactivate** an active card, **Activate** an inactive one, **Publish** a draft, **Duplicate** the card into a new draft, or **Delete** it. See the [Stamp cards overview](/merchants/stamp-cards/overview) for those.

Editing a card is available to owners and managers. Staff do not have access to the Cards page by default.
