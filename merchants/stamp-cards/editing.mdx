You can change a stamp card after it goes live: rename it, adjust the design, update the rewards, or change the stamp goal. The editor is the same four-step wizard you used to create the card, opened in edit mode and pre-filled with the card's current settings.

To open it, go to **Cards** in the left sidebar and click the card you want to change. You can also click the three-dot menu on the card and choose **Edit**.

## What you can change

The editor has the same steps as the setup wizard: Card Design, Rewards, Locations, and Review. A live preview on the right updates as you type. Each field works the same way it does when you create a card, so the field references still apply:

- [Designing your stamp card](/merchants/stamp-cards/designing) for the look and the Card Design fields
- [Stamp card rewards](/merchants/stamp-cards/rewards) for the Rewards step
- [Advanced settings](/merchants/stamp-cards/advanced-settings) for stamping rules, expiry, and bonus stamps

The badge next to the title shows the card's current status: Active, Draft, or Inactive.

## Saving your changes

Move to the **Review** step when you are done. You have two ways to save:

- **Publish** writes your changes to the live card right away. Members keep collecting on it without a break.
- **Save as Draft** saves your changes but switches the card to Draft. A draft card is hidden from new members and stops issuing new stamps until you publish it again.

<Frame caption="On Review, Publish updates the live card. Save as Draft saves your changes but takes the card offline until you publish again.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/stamp-cards/editing-03-review.png" alt="The Review step showing the card summary, configured rewards, and the Save as Draft and Publish actions" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
Use **Save as Draft** carefully on a card members are already using. It takes the card offline, so customers cannot earn new stamps on it until you publish again. If you only want to keep an active card live, use **Publish**.
</Warning>

## How edits affect members already collecting

A few changes carry over to members who already have stamps:

- **Changing the stamp goal applies to everyone right away.** If you raise the goal from 10 to 12, a member sitting at 9 stamps now works toward 12. Their existing stamps are not reset, so nobody loses progress, but the finish line moves for everyone on the card.
- **Saving rebuilds the card's reward list.** When you save, Qtap replaces the card's rewards with whatever is in the Rewards step. If you remove a reward there, it comes off the card. Add or rename rewards in that step to change them.
- **You cannot switch a card to a points program.** The card type is fixed once it exists. To offer a different model, create a new card or [points program](/merchants/points/creating) instead.

<Tip>
To take a card offline or bring it back without opening the editor, use the three-dot menu on the [Cards page](/merchants/stamp-cards/overview): Deactivate, Activate, or Publish a draft straight from the list.
</Tip>

## Who can edit cards

Owners and managers can edit stamp cards. Staff cannot by default, since the Cards section is off for the Staff role unless you grant access. See [Roles and permissions](/merchants/staff/roles-permissions) for how to change that.
