---
title: "Filtering Analytics by Location"
description: "How to compare numbers across branches and drill into one branch at a time."
---

With two or more locations, the Analytics page has tools for comparing branches side by side and for looking at one branch in isolation. This page covers both.

## The Locations tab

Go to **Analytics** in the left sidebar and click the **Locations** tab. You'll see a table with one row per location, comparing every key number across branches. See [Location Comparison](/merchants/analytics/location-comparison) for what each column means.

## Filtering the main dashboard to one location

The main Analytics page has a **Location** dropdown at the top right. By default it's set to **All locations**. Pick a single location from the dropdown and every chart, KPI, and table on the page rebuilds to show only that branch's data.

The filter persists as you click between Analytics tabs (Stamps, Points, Staff, Revenue Impact). Clear it by picking **All locations** again or by clicking the X next to the location name.

## What each filter affects

The location filter applies to:

- KPIs at the top of the page (members, stamps, redemptions, etc.).
- All charts.
- The Staff Performance table (only shows staff assigned to the selected location).
- The Customer Segments breakdown.

It does not affect:

- The total member count on your account (members aren't location-specific).
- Billing-related numbers.

## Looking at one location's day-by-day numbers

With the location filter set, change the time period selector to **7d** for the last week, or pick a custom date range. This is the fastest way to investigate a slow week at a single branch.

## Exporting a single-location report

Click **Export** at the top right with the location filter applied. The CSV or PDF that downloads only contains the selected location's data. This is the report to attach to a quarterly review with a branch manager.
