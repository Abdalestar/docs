---
title: "Adding and Editing Locations"
description: "Every field on the location form, what it does, and what to put in it."
---

Go to **Settings → Locations** in the left sidebar. Click **Add Location** to open the form.

## Required fields

These have to be filled in before you can save.

- **Business Name.** Up to 100 characters. This is what shows on the customer's app and on your merchant page. If all your branches are called “Your Cafe,” include the area in the name (“Your Cafe — City Centre”, “Your Cafe — Pearl”) so members know which is which.
- **Address Line 1.** Street and building number.
- **City.** Just the city.
- **State or Region.**
- **Postal Code.**
- **Country.** Pick from the dropdown.

## Optional but worth filling in

- **Address Line 2.** For unit, suite, or floor numbers.
- **Phone.** Shown on your merchant page so customers can call.
- **Hours of Operation.** Set Open and Close times for each day, or mark a day Closed. Also shown on your merchant page.

## Map and coordinates

As you type the address, Google Maps suggests matches below the field. Click a suggestion. The Latitude and Longitude fields fill in automatically and a small map preview appears.

If the map preview is wrong, drag the pin on the map to the right spot. The coordinates update as you drag.

<Note>
  Accurate coordinates matter if you ever want to use location-aware features later, like “only count scans within 100 metres of the branch.” For now, the coordinates are mainly used for showing the location on your merchant page.
</Note>

## Active toggle

The **Active Location** switch is on by default. Leave it on. The only reason to switch a brand-new location off is if you're setting it up before opening day and don't want it visible yet.

## Saving

Click **Save Location**. The location is created immediately and you're taken back to the Locations list.

## Editing a location

Click **Edit** on the row of the location you want to change. Every field can be edited. Save your changes by clicking **Save Location** at the bottom of the form.

Changes take effect immediately:

- Name and hours update on your merchant page within a minute.
- The address change applies to all future stamps and redemptions at this location.
- Past stamps and redemptions keep the old address on record. They are not retroactively rewritten.

## Deleting a location

Click **Delete** on the row. Qtap shows a confirmation dialog. Type the location name to confirm.

<Warning>
  Deleting a location does not delete the members or stamps that were earned there. Those records stay on the account, but the location field on those records becomes “Deleted location.” This makes the location filter in Analytics less useful for old data.
</Warning>

If the location was bought as an extra-location add-on, deleting it does not cancel the add-on. Cancel the add-on separately from **Settings → Billing**.
