Your plan comes with a set number of locations, staff seats, loyalty cards, QR codes, campaigns, and push notifications. The Billing page shows a meter for each one, so you can see how much you have used and how much is left. Open **Settings > Billing** and stay on the **Overview** tab.

  <img src="/images/merchants/billing/usage-meters-overview.png" alt="Billing Overview tab showing six usage meters for locations, staff, loyalty cards, QR codes, campaigns, and push notifications" />
</Frame>

## What each meter counts

Every meter reads **X of Y used**, where Y is your plan's limit. Each one counts something different:

- **Locations:** your active locations. A location you have deactivated does not count.
- **Staff Members:** your active staff accounts. Anyone you have removed does not count.
- **Loyalty Cards:** your active stamp cards and points programs added together. A salon with one stamp card and one points program uses two.
- **QR Codes (this month):** the QR codes you have generated since the first of the current month.
- **Campaigns:** every campaign in your account.
- **Push Notifications (this month):** the pushes you have sent since the first of the current month.

Two of these reset. The **QR Codes** and **Push Notifications** meters start again from zero on the first of each month, so a full QR meter clears itself when the next month begins. The other four are running totals that change only when you add or remove something.

## When a meter turns red

A meter fills as you get closer to the limit. Once you reach it, the count and the bar turn red so you can spot it at a glance. The number stays a plain fraction the whole time, so the figure you read is always the real one.

<Frame caption="A meter at or over its limit turns red.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/billing/usage-meters-over-limit.png" alt="The Campaigns meter reading 8 of 5 used in red" />
</Frame>

The Campaigns meter can read over its limit, like 8 of 5, when you have more campaigns saved than your current plan allows. You can still run the campaigns you already have, but you cannot start a new one until you are back under the limit. The same rule applies across the board: while a meter sits at its limit, you cannot add another location, invite more staff, or create another campaign until you free up room or raise the limit.

## What to do when one fills up

You have three ways to make room:

- **Upgrade your plan** to raise the limit on everything at once.
- **Buy a single add-on** from the Add-ons tab if you only need more of one thing, like an extra location or an extra loyalty card.
- **Wait or remove items.** The two monthly meters clear on the first. For the running totals, deactivate a location, remove a staff member, or delete a campaign you no longer run.

Buying an add-on raises that meter's limit right away, so an extra-location add-on moves your Locations meter from 5 of 5 to 5 of 6.

<Info>
On a plan with unlimited limits, a meter shows the count next to an infinity sign and no bar, since there is nothing to fill up.
</Info>

The meters live under Settings, so the account owner always sees them, and a manager or staff member sees them only with Settings access.
