The Merchants page is your business-at-a-glance view. It shows the profile card that represents your business in Qtap, along with live loyalty stats pulled from your member data. You can preview your public page, jump to the editor, or delete the profile from here.

## What you see on this page

Your profile card displays the information you entered in the [Merchant Page editor](/merchants/settings/merchant-page):

- **Business name** and an "Active" badge
- **Logo** (or a placeholder icon if you haven't uploaded one)
- **Description**, if you added one in the editor
- **Category and location** (for example, "Cafe" and "The Pearl, Qatar")
- **Phone number and website**
- **Google rating**, if you entered one
- **Loyalty card count** showing how many stamp or points cards are linked to your profile

Below the profile details, you see live stats calculated from your actual member data:

- **Members** counts everyone who has joined your loyalty program
- **Stamps earned** is the total stamps collected across all members
- **Points earned** is the total points accumulated across all members
- **Redeemed** is the total number of reward redemptions

These numbers update each time you open the page. They pull directly from your member records, so they always reflect the current state of your program.

## If you don't have a profile yet

When you first open the Merchants page, you see an empty state with a "Create Merchant" button. Clicking it takes you to the [Merchant Page editor](/merchants/settings/merchant-page) where you fill in your business details, upload a logo, add business hours, and connect social links.

Once you save, the profile card appears on this page automatically.

## Preview your public page

If you have set a URL slug in the editor, a **Preview** button appears on the profile card. Clicking it opens your public-facing merchant page in a new tab. This is the page your customers see when they visit your Qtap link.

Use this to check how your business name, logo, description, and loyalty cards look to customers before sharing the link.

## Edit your profile

Click **Edit Merchant** at the top right (or the **Edit** button on the card) to go to the Merchant Page editor. Changes you make there show up on this overview page after you save.

For a full walkthrough of every field in the editor, see [Merchant Page settings](/merchants/settings/merchant-page).

## Delete your profile

The **Delete** button removes your cover photo, logo, and all merchant page settings. A confirmation dialog appears first, since this action cannot be undone. Deleting the profile does not affect your loyalty programs, members, or transaction history.

<Warning>
Deleting your merchant profile removes your public page and all visual branding. Your loyalty data stays intact, but customers will no longer be able to view your business page through its Qtap link.
</Warning>

## Who can access this page

Only **owners** can view the Merchants page and the Merchant Page editor. Managers and staff do not have access, regardless of their other permissions. If a non-owner tries to visit this page, they see an "Access Denied" message.
