The Members page does two housekeeping jobs beyond viewing and searching: it hands you a spreadsheet of your members, and it removes members you no longer want on your program. Both start from **Members** in the left sidebar. For the list, search, and sort controls, see [the Members overview](/merchants/members/overview).

## Export your members to a spreadsheet

Click **Export CSV** at the top right of the Members page. Qtap builds a `.csv` file of every member in your organization, across all pages, and downloads it to your computer as `members-` plus today's date.

<Frame caption="The Export CSV button downloads your whole member list. Member names and contact details are covered here for privacy.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/members/01-export-csv.png" alt="Members page with the Export CSV button highlighted" />
</Frame>

The file opens in Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets. Each member is one row, with these columns:

| Column | What it holds |
| --- | --- |
| First Name, Last Name | The member's name, if they gave one |
| Email, Phone | Their contact details |
| Birthday | Used for birthday campaigns |
| Join Date | When they first joined your program |
| Total Stamps, Total Points | Lifetime totals earned with you |
| Visit Count, Last Visit | Counted from the member's scan and reward history |

Only owners and managers can export. A team member on the Staff role does not see the Export button.

### Export only some members

To save a smaller list, tick the checkbox on each member's row. A toolbar appears with **Export Selected**, which downloads only the rows you picked as `selected-members-` plus today's date.

<Frame caption="Select a few members to export or act on just those rows.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/members/02-export-selected.png" alt="Two members selected, with the Export Selected and Delete buttons highlighted in the bulk toolbar" />
</Frame>

## Delete members

Deleting takes a member off your program. You can remove one member or several at once:

- **One member:** open the three-dot menu at the end of their row and choose **Delete Member**.
- **Several members:** tick their checkboxes, then click **Delete** in the toolbar that appears.

Either way, Qtap asks you to confirm first.

<Frame caption="The confirmation step before any member is removed.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/members/03-delete-confirm.png" alt="Permanently Remove Members dialog with a Cancel button and a red Permanently Delete button" />
</Frame>

The confirmation spells out what goes: every stamp, point, redemption, and activity record that member built up with your business. Click **Permanently Delete** to finish, or **Cancel** to back out. While the delete runs, the button reads "Deleting...".

What you remove is the member's place on *your* program and their loyalty history with you. Their Qtap account stays intact, so if they scan one of your codes again later, they join again as a fresh member with no stamps or points from before.

<Warning>
Deleting a member cannot be undone. There is no trash or restore. If you might want the record later, export the member first. Owners can always delete; managers can too. A team member on the Staff role cannot delete members unless you give them full member access.
</Warning>
