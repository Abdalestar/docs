This is the page to send to a new staff member on their first shift. It walks through the screen they'll use to redeem a customer's reward.

## Before you start

You need:

- A Qtap login. Your account owner sends an invite to your email. Set a password the first time you log in.
- Access to the Qtap dashboard on a tablet, phone, or computer at the counter.

Go to **Redemptions** in the left sidebar.

## The redemption screen

The screen has two halves. The top half is for finding the customer. The bottom half shows their pending rewards once you've found them.

### Finding the customer

You have three ways:

1. **Scan their reward QR code.** Click the **Scan** button. Allow camera access if prompted. Point the camera at the customer's phone, where they have the reward open. The reward fills in automatically and you can skip to step 3.
2. **Type the 6-character code.** Every reward has a short code (for example, `ABCD12`). Type it into the **Redemption Code** field and press Enter.
3. **Search for the member.** Type the customer's name, phone, or email into **Look up member**. Pick them from the list of matches.

### Confirming the reward

Once the customer is found, the bottom of the screen shows their pending rewards. Each row has the reward name, the date it was issued, and how soon it expires.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick the right reward">
    The customer tells you what they're claiming. Click the row that matches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the staff note">
    Some rewards have a note from the owner reminding you what to give the customer. The note shows in a yellow box at the top of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hand over the item">
    Give the customer the actual reward (the free coffee, the discount, whatever it is).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Redeem">
    The button turns green and shows “Redeemed.” The reward disappears from the customer's app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What if the customer is offline

If the customer's phone has no signal and they can't open the app, you have two options:

1. Ask them for the email or phone number on their account, search for them in **Look up member**, and use the rewards list there. The customer doesn't need their phone.
2. If they don't remember which email they used, ask them to come back when they have signal. Don't redeem from a guess.

## A common mistake to avoid

Do not click **Redeem** before handing the item over. Once you click Redeem, the reward is gone from the customer's app. If you redeem first and then realise you don't have stock, you can't undo it. Reissue the reward yourself from **Members → [member] → Issue Reward** if needed.

## What to do at the end of a shift

Nothing. Redemptions save automatically. The Owner sees your activity in **Analytics → Staff** at any time.
