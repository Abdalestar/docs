title: "Redemptions Overview"
description: "What a redemption is, how it shows up in the dashboard, and who can redeem rewards."
---

A redemption is the moment a customer uses a reward they've earned. They show the reward in their app, your staff verifies the code, and the reward is marked as used. The Redemptions section of the dashboard is where staff handle this from your end.

## When a redemption happens

Three things lead to a customer having a reward to redeem:

1. They reached the **stamp goal** on a stamp card.
2. They received a reward from a **campaign** (birthday, win-back, milestone).
3. They got a **sign-up reward** when they joined a stamp card.

In all three cases, the reward sits in the customer's app under “My Rewards” until they redeem it or it expires.

<Note>
  Points-based redemptions (spending points on a reward) are being built. For now, only stamp card and campaign rewards can be redeemed through the dashboard. We'll update this page when points redemption is live.
</Note>

## What the dashboard shows

Go to **Redemptions** in the left sidebar. The page has two main areas:

- **Pending Rewards** — rewards that have been issued but not yet redeemed.
- **Redemption History** — every reward that has been redeemed, with date, staff member, and location.

Use the date picker, location filter, and member search at the top of the page to narrow either list.

## Who can redeem

Any staff member with a Qtap account can run a redemption. Staff don't need any special permission. The role only changes how much else of the dashboard they can see.

- **Owner** and **Manager** can redeem at any location.
- **Staff** can redeem at the locations they're assigned to. If they try to redeem at a different location, the redemption is blocked.

## What gets recorded

Every redemption stores:

- The member's name and qtap_id
- The reward name and value
- The exact date and time
- Which staff member ran the redemption
- Which location it happened at
- The redemption code that was used

You can see all of this in the Redemption History table and on the member's profile page.
