The Billing page in Settings is the home of everything money. Plan changes, add-ons, payment method, and invoice history all live here. Go to **Settings → Billing** in the left sidebar.

<Note>
  Only the account Owner can change the plan or update the payment method. Managers and staff can view this page if their account has the settings permission turned on.
</Note>

## What you'll see on the page

The page has four sections.

### Current Plan

Shows the name of your active plan, the monthly or annual price, and a status badge: **Active**, **Trial**, **Past Due**, or **Canceled**. Below the badge you'll see your next billing date.

If you're on a trial, the badge shows the number of days left.

### Usage Meters

A list of how much of each plan limit you've used this month:

- **Locations** — X of Y locations used
- **Staff** — X of Y staff seats used
- **Loyalty Cards** — X of Y programs active
- **QR Code Scans This Month** — X of Y allowed
- **Active Campaigns** — X of Y
- **Push Notifications This Month** — X of Y
- **AI Credits Remaining**
- **Batch QR Credits Remaining**

When you hit a limit, the meter turns red and a link to **Upgrade Plan** appears next to it.

### Active Add-Ons

Lists any extras you've bought on top of your plan: extra locations, extra cards, custom QR branding, NFC tags, AI insight credits. Each row shows the price and a **Cancel** button for recurring add-ons.

### Payment History

A table of past invoices with date, amount, and status (Paid or Failed). The download icon on the right lets you save a PDF receipt.

## Where to do common things

- **Change plan** — click **Upgrade Plan** or **Downgrade Plan**. See [Upgrading and Downgrading](/merchants/billing/upgrading-and-downgrading).
- **Buy add-ons** — click **Add-Ons** at the top of the page. See [Add-Ons](/merchants/billing/add-ons).
- **Update card** — click **Manage Subscription**. This opens the Stripe Customer Portal in a new tab. See [Payment Methods](/merchants/billing/payment-methods).
- **Cancel** — click **Manage Subscription** and use the Cancel option there. See [Refunds and Cancellation](/merchants/billing/refunds-and-cancellation).
