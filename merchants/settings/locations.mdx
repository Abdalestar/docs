title: "Locations"
description: "Add and edit the physical branches where your loyalty program runs."
---

Locations are the physical addresses your business operates from. Each loyalty card, each QR code, and each redemption is tied to a location. Go to **Settings → Locations** in the left sidebar.

<Note>
  Your plan limits how many locations you can have. If you've hit the limit, the **Add Location** button is disabled and a link to upgrade appears in its place.
</Note>

## What you'll see

The page is a list of every location on your account. For each one, you'll see:

- **Business Name** — what shows on the customer's app and on the merchant page.
- **Address** — the full street address.
- **Status badge** — **Active** (green) or **Inactive** (gray). Inactive locations don't accept stamps and aren't visible to customers.
- **Edit** and **Delete** buttons.

## Adding your first location

Click **Add Location** at the top right of the page. The form has these fields:

- **Business Name** — up to 100 characters.
- **Address Line 1** — required.
- **Address Line 2** — optional, for suite or unit numbers.
- **City**, **State**, **Postal Code**, **Country** — required.
- **Phone** — optional.
- **Hours of Operation** — set open and close times for each day, or mark a day as Closed.
- **Active Location** toggle — leave it on for the location to accept stamps.

As you type the address, Google Maps suggests matches. Click a suggestion and the latitude and longitude fill in automatically. A small map preview appears below the form.

Click **Save Location** to create it.

## Editing a location

Click **Edit** on the location row. You can change any field. When you save, future scans use the new values. Past stamps and redemptions keep the old location info on record.

## Deleting a location

Click **Delete**. Qtap asks you to confirm. Deleting a location removes it from the dropdown when staff issue stamps or redeem rewards. Members who joined at a deleted location are not removed.

<Warning>
  Deleting a location does not refund a billing add-on slot. If you bought an extra location and then delete it, you keep paying for the add-on until you cancel it from **Settings → Billing**.
</Warning>

## Setting one location as the default

If you have more than one, click the star icon on a location row to mark it as default. New loyalty cards, new staff, and new QR codes start with this location pre-selected.
