The Business Profile page in Settings is where Qtap stores the public and operational details for your business. Open **Settings** in the sidebar, then click **Business Profile** (the first tab at the top of the page).

The page has five sections, and each one saves independently. Changes you make in one section have no effect on the others until you click that section's own save button.

## Business logo

Your logo appears in the customer app and on your public Qtap merchant page. Click **Upload Logo** to pick a file from your computer. Qtap uploads it the moment you select it — no separate save step.

<Tip>
Use a square image at 400×400 px or larger. A PNG or JPG both work. Logos that are significantly wider or taller than they are square may appear cropped inside the app.
</Tip>

## Business information

This section has four fields: Business Name, Business Email, Phone Number, and Website. Business Name is required. The other three are optional.

Click **Save Changes** when you are done. The button is greyed out until you actually change something, so if it looks disabled, the form still has its original values.

## Business details

These fields affect how Qtap formats and categorizes information for your account.

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| Country | Your country of operation |
| Currency | The currency shown on your merchant page. Default is QAR. |
| VAT / Tax Percentage | Your VAT rate, stored for reference |
| Google Rating | Your current rating, displayed on your public merchant page |
| Industry | How your business is categorized |
| Industry sub-category | Becomes available after you pick an industry |
| Time zone | Controls how timestamps appear throughout your dashboard. Default is Asia/Qatar. |

Click **Save Details** when done.

<Info>
The VAT / Tax Percentage field stores a number for display purposes. It does not automatically calculate tax on transactions.
</Info>

## Business hours

Set your opening and closing times for each day of the week. If a day is closed, turn on the **Closed** toggle — the time pickers for that day go grey and are ignored.

Click **Save Hours** when done.

## Social links

Add your URLs for Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter), TikTok, and Snapchat. All five are optional. They appear on your public-facing Qtap merchant page so customers can find your accounts.

Click **Save Social Links** when done.

## Who can access settings

Only account owners can view and update the Business Profile page. Staff members who navigate to `/settings` see an access denied screen.

<Warning>
The **Delete Account** option at the bottom of the page is not yet active. It shows as a placeholder and cannot be clicked.
</Warning>
