Qtap can notify you when members join, redeem rewards, or when stock is running low. You control which events send email notifications and which send push notifications separately.

<Frame caption="Settings → Notifications: separate email and push toggles, with Save Preferences in the top right.">
  <img src="/images/settings/settings-notifications-overview.png" alt="The Notification Preferences page showing the Email Notifications and Push Notifications cards and the Save Preferences button" />
</Frame>

Changes don't save automatically. Click **Save Preferences** after making changes. The button stays grayed out until you've changed something.

## Email notifications

Go to **Settings → Notifications** to find the Email Notifications section.

<Frame caption="The Email Notifications card with its three toggles.">
  <img src="/images/settings/settings-notifications-email.png" alt="Email Notifications card showing New Member Sign-ups, Reward Redemptions, and Weekly Report toggles" />
</Frame>

There are three email toggles:

**New Member Sign-ups** — sends an email each time a member joins your program. Off by default.

**Reward Redemptions** — sends an email when a member uses a reward. Off by default.

**Weekly Report** — sends a summary of your program's performance every week. On by default.

## Push notifications

Push notifications arrive in your browser or on your phone if you've allowed notifications for Qtap.

<Frame caption="The Push Notifications card with its three toggles.">
  <img src="/images/settings/settings-notifications-push.png" alt="Push Notifications card showing New Member Sign-ups, Reward Redemptions, and Low Stock Alerts toggles" />
</Frame>

There are three push toggles:

**New Member Sign-ups** — notifies you as members join. Off by default.

**Reward Redemptions** — notifies you when a reward is redeemed. On by default.

**Low Stock Alerts** — notifies you when a reward's stock is getting low. On by default.

<Note>
Your browser needs permission to show push notifications. If push alerts aren't arriving, check that you haven't blocked Qtap in your browser's notification settings.
</Note>

## Save your preferences

After changing any toggle, click **Save Preferences** in the top right. A small banner at the bottom of the screen appears while you have unsaved changes.
