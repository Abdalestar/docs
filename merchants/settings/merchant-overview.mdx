The Merchants page shows your public business profile on Qtap along with live totals from your loyalty program. Go to **Settings → Merchants** in the sidebar to get there.

Only the account owner can view this page. Managers and staff see an access denied message.

<Frame caption="Left: the empty state before you create a profile. Right: the card view once your profile is live.">
  <img src="/images/settings/merchants-states.svg" alt="Merchants page in two states: empty and with an active profile" />
</Frame>

## If you haven't created a profile

You'll land on an empty state with a **Create Merchant** button. Clicking it opens the setup form where you can add your business details, logo, cover photo, business hours, and social links.

## When you have a profile

Your profile appears as a card showing your logo, business name, category, location, phone, and website. Below those details, four numbers update as your loyalty programs run:

- **Members**: total customers enrolled across all your programs
- **Stamps earned**: lifetime total across all your stamp cards
- **Points earned**: lifetime total across all your points programs
- **Redeemed**: total redemptions across all programs

The stats come from your member records. They update each time a customer earns or redeems and only appear once at least one member has joined.

<Frame caption="The merchant profile card showing live loyalty stats.">
  <img src="/images/settings/merchants-overview-final.png" alt="Merchant profile card with stats" />
</Frame>

On the right side of the card, **Preview** opens the customer-facing version of your page in a new tab so you can see what your members see. **Edit** (and the matching **Edit Merchant** button at the top of the page) opens the full settings form. **Delete** removes your profile permanently.

<Info>
  The **Active** badge on your card is a status label, not a toggle. There is no way to temporarily hide or unpublish your merchant page without deleting it.
</Info>

## Deleting your profile

The delete button asks for confirmation before doing anything. Once confirmed, Qtap removes your cover photo, logo, and all merchant page settings. Your loyalty programs, members, and transaction history stay in place. Only the public profile is removed.

If you want to take your listing down temporarily, deleting is the only option. You can recreate it later from the same page.
