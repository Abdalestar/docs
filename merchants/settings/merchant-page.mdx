Your merchant page is the public website Qtap hosts for your business. It's where customers land when they scan a QR code or follow a link from your social profiles. Go to **Settings → Merchant Page** in the left sidebar.

## What the page is for

The merchant page does three jobs:

1. Tells customers who you are and what your loyalty program is.
2. Lets new customers join your program (sign up).
3. Lists your locations and hours.

The URL is `qtap.io/m/[your-slug]` by default. You can set a custom domain on the Elite plan and above.

## Fields you can edit

The page is one long form. Save your changes at the bottom.

### Branding

- **Business Name** — shown as the page title.
- **Tagline** — one short line under the name (for example, “Freshly roasted in Doha”).
- **Logo** — a square logo, PNG or JPG, up to 2MB.
- **Hero Image** — the background image at the top of the page. Use a wide image, up to 5MB.
- **Brand Color** — used for buttons and accents. Pick a color that contrasts with your logo.

### About

- **About Section** — a few sentences about your business. Plain text, supports basic formatting.
- **Contact Email** — shown publicly. Customers can use it to reach you outside the app.
- **Phone** — optional, shown on the page.

### Social

Links to your social profiles. Leave blank to hide.

- **Instagram URL**
- **TikTok URL**
- **Facebook URL**

### Custom Domain

Available on Elite and Franchise plans. Enter your domain (for example, `loyalty.yourbusiness.com`) and Qtap shows the DNS records to point at us. The change goes live within an hour of DNS propagating.

## Live preview

A preview of the page appears on the right side of the screen as you edit. The preview updates as you type, so you can see exactly what customers will see.

## Publishing

Click **Save Changes** at the bottom to publish. The page is live immediately at your URL. There's no separate publish step.

<Warning>
  Logos with a transparent background work best, since the page background may be light or dark depending on the customer's device theme.
</Warning>
