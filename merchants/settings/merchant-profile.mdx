Go to **Merchants** in the sidebar to see how your business appears on the Qtap customer app, and to preview, edit, or remove your public profile. Each organization has one merchant profile.

## Before you create a profile

If you haven't set one up yet, the page shows a placeholder with a **Create Merchant** button. Click it and you'll land on the profile form where you enter your business name, description, category, location, phone, website, and social links. Once you save, you come back to the Merchants page and your card appears.

## What the card shows

The card has two sections.

Your business details include your logo, business name, an Active badge, description, category, location name, phone, website, and Google rating. Any field you left blank during setup won't appear on the card.

Below the details, a row of live stats pulls directly from your member data:

- Members enrolled in your loyalty program
- Stamps issued across all members
- Points earned across all members
- Rewards redeemed

Stats only appear when a count is above zero. If your program is new, the stats row won't show yet.

## Actions

**Preview** opens your public page in a new tab. The link goes to `qtap.qa/m/[your-slug]` with a preview flag so you can check how it looks without it counting as a customer visit. Useful for sharing with your team before you announce your loyalty program.

**Edit** takes you to the profile form where you can update any field, swap your logo, or change your hours.

**Delete** removes your merchant profile. A confirmation dialog appears before anything changes.

<Warning>
Deleting removes your logo, cover photo, and all profile settings. Your organization, staff, members, and loyalty programs are not affected. There is no undo, but you can create a new profile at any time.
</Warning>

## Who can access this page

Owners and managers can view and manage the Merchants page. Staff members see an access-denied screen.

<Info>
To edit your profile fields (logo, hours, currency, social links, and more), see [Merchant page settings](/merchants/settings/merchant-page).
</Info>
