The Points Expiry settings decide whether points last forever or run out after a while. By default they never expire, so a member keeps every point until they spend it. Turn expiry on when you want points to stay active only for a set window.

You set this on the points program itself, in the **Points Expiry** card. It is there when you create a program and when you edit one later.

## Turn on expiry

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the program form">
    Go to **Points** in the sidebar, then create a new program or open an existing one to edit. Scroll to the **Points Expiry** card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Points Expiry">
    Switch on **Enable Points Expiry**. Two fields appear below it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the expiry period">
    In **Expiry Period (days)**, enter how many days points stay valid. The longest you can set is 365 days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an expiry type">
    Pick how the countdown is measured. The line under the fields restates your choice in plain words.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the program">
    Save or activate the program. Expiry applies from then on.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The three expiry types

<Frame caption="The Expiry Type dropdown: From date earned, From last activity, or End of calendar year.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/points/expiry-03-types.png" alt="The Expiry Type dropdown open, listing the three options" />
</Frame>

| Expiry type | What expires | When it goes |
|---|---|---|
| From date earned | Each batch of points on its own clock | The set number of days after those points were earned |
| From last activity | The member's whole balance | The set number of days after their last earn or redemption |
| End of calendar year | Everything earned during the year | At the start of the next year |

**From date earned** gives each earn its own expiry date. Points a member earned in March expire before points the same member earned in June.

**From last activity** keeps points alive as long as the member keeps coming back. Every time they earn or redeem, the clock resets. Points run out only after a full quiet period with no activity.

**End of calendar year** clears balances once a year. Points earned during a year expire at the start of the following year, whatever the day count says.

## How and when points expire

Expiry runs on its own. A background job checks your programs once a day and removes points that have reached their expiry date. You don't trigger it, and it looks only at active programs that have expiry switched on.

When points expire for a member:

- Their balance drops by the expired amount. It never goes below zero, and points they already spent are not counted again.
- An **expired** entry is added to their points history, so you can see what came off and when.
- The member gets a notification with the number of points that expired (a push message, or an email if push is off).

<Note>
**From date earned** stamps an expiry date on each point as it is earned, so it applies to points earned after you turn the setting on. Points a member earned beforehand have no expiry date and stay on their balance. The other two types work from the member's balance and activity, so they also cover older points.
</Note>

## Change or turn off expiry

Edit the program at any time to change the period or the type, or switch **Enable Points Expiry** off to stop future expiry.
