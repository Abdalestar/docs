Points Operations is where your staff award points at the counter and fix balance mistakes. The page has three tabs: **Award Points**, **Adjust/Deduct**, and **Recent Activity**. You need an active points program before anything on this page works.

<Info>
Staff need the **Award points** permission to use this page. You can grant it in **Staff → Roles & Permissions**.
</Info>

## Award points for a purchase

The **Award Points** tab calculates how many points a transaction earns based on your program's rate. Search for the member, enter the amount, and confirm.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for the member">
    Type the member's name, email, or phone number in the search field and select them from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the transaction amount">
    Type the purchase amount in the **Transaction Amount** field. The preview updates as you type to show exactly how many points this amount earns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an optional note">
    Use the **Note** field to record what the purchase was for. This appears in the transaction log.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the award">
    Click **Award Points** and confirm in the dialog. Points are added to the member's balance right away, and a success screen shows their new total.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If your points program has a minimum transaction amount, purchases below it calculate as 0 points. The preview shows this before you confirm.
</Note>

### Award a specific number of points instead

If you want to give a set number of points rather than calculate from an amount, switch to **Manual Points** using the toggle above the transaction field. Type the exact points to award and skip the amount calculation.

## Adjust/Deduct a member's balance

Use the **Adjust/Deduct** tab to add or remove points outside a normal transaction. Common reasons: fixing a data entry error, honoring a one-off exception, or reversing a duplicate award.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the member">
    Search for and select the member whose balance you want to change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Add or Deduct">
    Click **Add Points** or **Deduct Points**. The button changes color to make the direction clear before you confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount and reason">
    Type the number of points and fill in the **Reason** field. The reason is required and is saved to the transaction history so other staff can see why the change was made.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click the action button and confirm in the dialog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Deductions send the member a push notification or email. There is no way to undo a balance adjustment after you confirm, so double-check the amount and direction first.
</Warning>

## Recent Activity

The **Recent Activity** tab shows the last 20 points transactions across all your members. Each row shows the member, transaction type, points, amount, description, and date.

Use this tab to quickly confirm a recent award went through or to spot an unexpected transaction.
