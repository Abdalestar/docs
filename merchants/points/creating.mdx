Open **Points** in the left sidebar and click **Create Program**. The form opens with a live card preview beside it, so you can see how your program will look to members as you fill it in.

<Frame caption="On the Points page, click Create Program to start a new points program.">

</Frame>

<Info>
Owners and managers can create a points program. Staff do not have access to this page by default.
</Info>

<Note>
Points programs and stamp cards share one plan limit. If you have reached your loyalty card limit, this page shows a message with a link to upgrade under **Settings > Billing** instead of the form.
</Note>

## Program details

Start with the basics at the top of the form:

1. **Program Name**: the name members see on their card in the app. Required.
2. **Points per**: how many points a customer earns for each unit of currency. Set it to 2 and a customer who spends 10 QAR earns 20 points. Defaults to 1.
3. **Currency**: the currency your prices are in. Defaults to QAR.
4. **Description**: a short line shown on the card, like "Earn 1 point for every QAR you spend."

Under **Appearance**, pick a **Card Color**. The preview on the right updates as you choose, so you can match your brand.

## Rewards

Rewards are what members redeem their points for. Each reward has its own points cost, so you can offer a small reward early and a bigger one further along.

<Steps>

### Click Add Reward

This opens the reward editor.

### Fill in the reward

- **Reward name**: what the customer gets, like "Free Coffee" or "Medium Latte".
- **Points required**: the number of points needed to redeem it, like 100.
- **Reward image**: an optional PNG or JPEG up to 1MB, shown next to the reward in the app.
- **Staff note**: an internal note for your team at redemption time. Members never see it.

### Save the reward

Click **Save reward**. Add as many rewards as you like, each at its own points threshold.

</Steps>

## Locations

Under **Locations**, choose the branches where members can earn points on this program. Leave every branch unchecked to make it valid at all of them. If you have not added any branches yet, add them first under **Settings > Locations**.

## Points expiry

By default, points never expire. To add an expiry rule, turn on **Enable Points Expiry** and set:

- **Expiry Period (days)**: how long points last.
- **Expiry Type**: count from the date earned, from the member's last activity, or to the end of the calendar year.

## Availability period

Leave this off and members can join the program at any time. To run it only for a set window, turn on **Limited Availability** and set an **Available From** and **Available Until** date. Members can earn points only between those dates.

## Limits

Use **Max points per visit** to cap how many points a customer can earn in a single transaction. Leave it empty for no cap.

<Frame caption="Set a per-visit points cap, or leave it empty for no limit.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/points/creating-05-limits.png" alt="The Limits card with the Max points per visit field set to no limit" />
</Frame>

## Terms and FAQs

Add any rules under **Terms & Conditions**. Members can read them from the card's terms sheet in the app.

Under **FAQs**, answer up to four common questions about the program. Each question and answer shows in the card's Questions section.

<Frame caption="Add up to four questions and answers that members can read on the card.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/points/creating-06-faqs.png" alt="The FAQs card with one question and answer filled in" />
</Frame>

## Saving and publishing

At the bottom of the form, click **Activate** to make the program live. Members can find and join it in the Qtap app right away.

Click **Save as Draft** to keep it private while you keep working. Draft programs are not visible to members.

<Frame caption="Save as Draft keeps the program private; Activate makes it live for members.">
  <img src="/images/merchants/points/creating-04-publish.png" alt="Bottom of the points program form showing the Save as Draft and Activate buttons" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
After a program is active and members have joined, you can still edit its name, description, and rewards. Changing the points per currency rate does not change points members have already earned.
</Warning>
