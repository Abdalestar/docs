A Welcome Campaign sends a single push notification to each new member after they sign up. You choose the delay: immediately, 1 hour, 24 hours (the default), or 48 hours. The campaign fires once per member and never repeats.

Cafes and salons use it to drop a first-visit voucher or a batch of bonus stamps into a new member's wallet, giving them a reason to return within their first week.

## Setting up a welcome campaign

Click **Campaigns** in the left sidebar, then click **New Campaign**. The wizard walks you through seven steps:

1. On the **Type** step, select **Welcome Campaign**. Give the campaign a name such as "Welcome Gift" or "New Member Offer", and an optional description. Click **Next**.

2. On the **Trigger** step, choose when to send the notification after signup: Immediately, 1 hour, 24 hours (default), or 48 hours. Click **Next**.

3. On the **Reward** step, optionally attach a reward. Members receive it in their wallet when the notification arrives. Click **Next** whether you add a reward or not.

4. On the **Message** step, write the notification title and body. Push notifications truncate on most phones after about 100 characters, so keep the body short. Click **Next**.

5. On the **Conditions** step, optionally narrow who receives the campaign. Setting a location filter, for example, limits the send to members who enrolled at that branch. Leave blank to send to all new members. Click **Next**.

6. On the **A/B Test** step, optionally set up two message variants to see which performs better. Click **Next**.

7. On the **Review** step, check the summary. Click **Activate** to start the campaign, or **Save as Draft** to return later.

<Note>
Each member receives the welcome notification once, and only if they joined while the campaign was active. Members who signed up before activation, or while the campaign was paused, will not receive it.
</Note>

## Choosing a delay

The default is 24 hours. That gives a new member time to finish setting up the app and enable push notifications on their device before the message arrives.

Sending immediately suits in-person signups where staff guide the customer through enrollment and want the reward to appear on the spot. A 48-hour delay works for programs where members tend to open the app a day or two after joining.

## Managing the campaign

After activating, the campaign appears on the Campaigns page with a status of **Active**. Click the three-dot menu on the campaign card to:

- **Pause**: stops welcome sends for members who join after that point
- **Edit**: change the delay, reward, or message
- **Delete**: removes the campaign permanently

<Warning>
Editing the delay after activation does not affect notifications already queued for members who signed up before the change.
</Warning>
