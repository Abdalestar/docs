Campaigns send automated messages to your members when specific conditions are met. You configure the trigger, an optional reward, and the notification message. After that, Qtap runs the campaign automatically.

## How campaigns work

When a member meets a campaign's trigger condition (for example, their birthday is in 3 days, or they haven't visited in 60 days), Qtap sends them a push notification. You can attach a reward to the notification so they receive something when they open it.

Each campaign runs on its own rules. You can have multiple campaigns active at the same time.

## Campaign types

Qtap has seven campaign types:

**Birthday Rewards** — sends a message a set number of days before a member's birthday. You choose how many days in advance.

**Win-Back Campaign** — targets members who haven't visited in a certain number of days. You set how many days of inactivity triggers the campaign.

**Milestone Celebration** — triggers when a member reaches a specific number of stamps or points. Useful for recognizing loyal customers at a key threshold.

**Time-Based Promotion** — sends messages on specific days of the week within a time window. Good for happy hours or recurring weekly offers.

**Welcome Campaign** — sends a message to new members after a delay you set, measured in hours after they join.

**Points Multiplier** — increases the points members earn during a set date range. Only available on a points program.

**Flash Sale** — a limited-time offer with a set duration. You define the discount or bonus, the duration in hours, and an optional cap on how many times it can be redeemed.

## Campaign statuses

- **Draft** — saved but not running yet. Members won't receive anything.
- **Active** — running. Qtap is monitoring for trigger conditions.
- **Paused** — temporarily stopped. No new notifications will be sent until you resume it.
- **Ended** — completed campaigns that are no longer running.

## Creating a campaign

Click **New Campaign** from the Campaigns page. A multi-step wizard walks you through:

1. Choosing the campaign type and giving it a name
2. Configuring the trigger
3. Optionally adding a reward (free item, bonus stamps, bonus points, or a discount)
4. Writing the notification title and message body
5. Optionally setting conditions to narrow which members are targeted
6. Optionally setting up an A/B test with two message variants
7. Reviewing and either saving as a draft or activating immediately

## Pausing and resuming campaigns

On the Campaigns page, find the campaign card and open its menu. Select **Pause** to stop it temporarily, or **Resume** to start it again. Paused campaigns keep all their settings.

## Plan limits

Your plan determines how many active campaigns you can run at once. If you've reached the limit, you'll see a message when trying to create a new one. Go to **Settings > Billing** to upgrade.

<Note>
Campaign notifications are sent to members via the Qtap member app. Members who haven't downloaded the app or enabled notifications won't receive them.
</Note>
