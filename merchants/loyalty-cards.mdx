The Stamp Cards page lists every stamp card your business has created. You reach it by clicking **Cards** in the dashboard sidebar. Each card appears as a visual preview (exactly how customers see it in the Qtap app) with a status badge and an action menu.

## Card statuses

Every stamp card sits in one of three states, shown as a colored badge at the top of each card.

| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| **Active** (green) | The card is live. Customers can see it, join it, and earn stamps. |
| **Draft** (amber) | Saved but not published. Only dashboard users can see it. |
| **Inactive** (gray) | Hidden from new customers. Existing members keep their stamps and can still redeem earned rewards, but no new stamps can be issued against this card. |

The tabs above the card grid (All, Active, Drafts, Inactive) filter the view. The count next to each tab tells you how many cards are in that state.

## Actions

Click the three-dot menu on any card to see the available actions. The options change depending on the card's current status.

| Action | When it appears |
|--------|----------------|
| **Edit** | Always — opens the full card settings |
| **Duplicate** | Always — creates a Draft copy with the same settings |
| **Publish** | Draft cards only — makes the card live for customers |
| **Deactivate** | Active cards only — takes the card offline; existing member data is kept |
| **Activate** | Inactive cards only — makes the card live again |
| **Convert to Draft** | Inactive cards only — returns to draft state for editing |
| **Delete** | Always — permanently removes the card |

You can also click directly on a card preview to go straight to the edit page.

<Warning>
Deleting a card cannot be undone. The dashboard will ask you to confirm before anything is removed.
</Warning>

<Info>
Editing an active card takes effect immediately. If you need to make changes without customers seeing them mid-update, deactivate the card first, make your edits, then activate it again.
</Info>

## Creating a new card

Click **Create Card** at the top right. This opens the card creation wizard, which walks you through design, rewards, locations, and a final review before the card goes live. For a step-by-step guide, see [Creating a Stamp Card](/merchants/stamp-cards/creating).

## Plan limits

Your billing plan caps how many stamp cards can be Active at the same time. If you've reached the limit, the Publish action will not appear in the menu. You can free up a slot by deactivating an existing card, or upgrade your plan in **Settings > Billing**.

## Points programs are separate

Points programs live in their own section. The Stamp Cards page only shows stamp-based programs. To create or manage a points program, use the **Points** section in the sidebar.
