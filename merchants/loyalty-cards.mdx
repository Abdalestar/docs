## Status tabs

Once you have at least one card, four tabs appear above the card grid. Each tab shows a count of the cards in that group.

**All** shows every card regardless of state. **Active** limits the view to cards that customers can currently earn stamps on. **Draft** shows cards you've saved but not published. **Inactive** shows cards you've paused.

The counts update as soon as you change a card's status.

## What each card shows

Each card in the grid displays a visual preview: the card color, stamp icon, name, and stamp goal. A status badge in the top-left corner of each card shows whether it's active, a draft, or inactive. Click anywhere on the card preview to open the editor.

## Card actions

The three-dot menu on each card opens a list of actions. What you see depends on the card's current status.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Draft cards">
    - **Edit** — opens the card editor
    - **Duplicate** — creates a draft copy with "(Copy)" appended to the name
    - **Publish** — makes the card live immediately
    - **Delete** — permanently removes the card and all associated data
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Active cards">
    - **Edit** — opens the card editor
    - **Duplicate** — creates a draft copy
    - **Deactivate** — pauses the card without deleting it
    - **Delete** — permanently removes the card and all associated data
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Inactive cards">
    - **Edit** — opens the card editor
    - **Duplicate** — creates a draft copy
    - **Activate** — puts the card back into service
    - **Convert to Draft** — moves the card back to draft for editing before relaunching
    - **Delete** — permanently removes the card and all associated data
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

When you **deactivate** a card, members who already have stamps keep their progress. They can still redeem any rewards they've already earned. New stamps cannot be issued against an inactive card.

**Duplicate** copies all settings across, including rewards and location restrictions. Use it when you want a new card based on an existing one without redoing the setup from scratch.

<Warning>
Deleting a card is permanent. Member stamp histories tied to that card are removed too. The confirmation dialog that appears before the delete goes through is the only warning you'll get.
</Warning>

## Creating a card

Click **Create Card** in the top right to open the four-step setup wizard. The wizard covers card design, rewards, location restrictions, and a final review before you publish.

<Frame caption="The card creation wizard, step one: card design.">
  <img src="/images/loyalty-cards/create-card-final.png" alt="Create stamp card wizard" />
</Frame>

Your plan limits how many active stamp cards you can have at once. If you've reached the limit, you'll see a message when you try to publish a draft. Go to **Settings → Billing** to upgrade.
